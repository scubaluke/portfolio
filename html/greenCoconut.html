Green Coconut Web Development

Growing your business

The landing page for Green Coconut web development is extremely dynamic, simple and clean. The tropical color scheme and layout entice a feeling of relaxation helping busy business owners more easily navigate the web development and technology world.

For this project I created a layout and generating a color scheme that really fit with the name of the business "Green Coconut". I kept the content simple and the layout clean, sometimes less is more. The important content is very easy to see and find, keeping users engaged. By conveying a sense of island calm I hope busy people can feel a sense of relaxation that draws them to the business and keeps them on the page.
